Privacy policy for foodcrawl



Apllication purpose and intent


This aplication's intention is to parse local restaurants for posts about today's menu. Finally the goal is to copy the link to post and provide it in Slack's group chat.

Data


The only data that will be held is a link to publicly accessible link on restaurant's Facebook page. No other data will be stored.

Cookies and other metadata


No cookies are stored or offered. The only metadata that might be held is an error message from Facebook's Graph API.
